About

Hello! I am Dr. Rukiye Savran Kızıltepe, currently working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Software Engineering at Ankara University. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Essex, United Kingdom. My research focuses on machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. I am passionate about developing AI technologies that can help solve real-world problems. Besides research, I enjoy collaborating on interdisciplinary projects and mentoring students interested in AI. Thank you for visiting my page!
